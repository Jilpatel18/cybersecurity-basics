## Definition
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, servers, networks, and data from digital attacks.
These attacks are usually intended to steal information, damage systems, or stop services from working.

## Why Is Cybersecurity Important?
Cybersecurity is important because almost everything today depends on technology.

Without cybersecurity:
- Personal data can be stolen
- Bank accounts can be hacked
- Websites and servers can be taken down
- Companies can lose money and trust

## Where Is Cybersecurity Used?
Cybersecurity is used in many places, such as:
- Banks and online payments
- Social media platforms
- Government systems
- Hospitals and healthcare systems
- Cloud servers and data centers

## Real-Life Example
If a hacker steals a user's login details from a website, they can:
- Access private data
- Change information
- Perform unauthorized actions

Cybersecurity helps prevent such attacks and protects users.

## Summary
Cybersecurity keeps our digital world safe by protecting data, systems, and networks from attackers.
